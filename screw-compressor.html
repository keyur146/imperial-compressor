Continuous Industrial Air Supply

Superior Efficiency & Performance

Rotary screw compressors provide continuous, pulsation-free airflow using twin-screw rotor technology—ideal for 24/7 manufacturing operations requiring reliable compressed air with low total cost of ownership.

Capacity: 100-3000+ CFM Power: 10-500 HP Pressure: 7, 10, 13 bar
Get Custom Quotation

How It Works

Twin intermeshing helical rotors compress air continuously as they rotate, delivering smooth airflow without pulsation—resulting in minimal vibration, compact design, and lower maintenance than reciprocating units.

  • Precision-machined rotors with optimized profiles
  • Integrated oil separation (<3 ppm carryover)
  • High-efficiency coolers for optimal temperatures

Performance Benefits

  • Designed for 24/7 continuous operation
  • Superior energy efficiency (kW per CFM)
  • Minimal vibration and quiet operation
  • Reduced maintenance vs. reciprocating units
  • Compact footprint with high output
