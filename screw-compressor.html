Continuous Industrial Air Supply

Superior Efficiency & Performance

Rotary screw compressors provide continuous, pulsation-free airflow using twin-screw rotor technology—ideal for 24/7 manufacturing operations requiring reliable compressed air with low total cost of ownership.

Get Custom Quotation

How It Works

Twin intermeshing helical rotors compress air continuously as they rotate, delivering smooth airflow without pulsation. This results in minimal vibration, a compact design, and lower maintenance requirements.

  • Precision-machined rotors with optimized profiles
  • Integrated oil separation for cleaner compressed air
  • High-efficiency coolers for optimal operating temperatures

Performance Benefits

  • Designed for reliable 24/7 continuous operation
  • Superior energy efficiency (kW per CFM)
  • Minimal vibration and quiet operation
  • Reduced maintenance requirements
  • Compact footprint with high output performance

Energy Savings

Our screw compressors are engineered to maximize energy efficiency, helping businesses significantly reduce operational costs.

  • VSD option offers 20–35% energy savings in variable-demand applications

Control Options

  • Modulation: Inlet valve adjustment for controlled airflow
  • Load/Unload: Efficient on/off cycling based on demand
  • Variable Speed Drive (VSD): 20–35% energy savings
  • Multi-compressor sequencing: Optimized performance across multiple units

Configurations

  • Air-Cooled: Simple installation with no external cooling required
  • Water-Cooled: Ideal for high-temperature environments
  • Tank-Mounted: Integrated storage for convenient operation
  • Dryer-Integrated: Provides ready-to-use compressed air

Applications

  • Pharmaceutical manufacturing
  • Food & beverage processing
  • Automotive manufacturing
  • Textile mills
  • Printing and packaging industries
