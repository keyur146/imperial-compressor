Lifecycle Support That Keeps Your Systems Running

Service Overview

Service That Keeps You Running

Compressed air failures don't just stop machines—they disrupt entire operations. Imperial Compressors provides complete lifecycle support for air and gas compression systems, backed by experienced engineers and fast response times.

01

Installation & Commissioning

Turnkey installation for new, upgraded, or replacement systems—handled end to end. Includes: site survey, piping & electrical setup, startup, performance validation, and operator training.

02

Onsite Repair & Maintenance

Fast, expert troubleshooting for reciprocating and screw compressors. Repairs include: valves, pistons, bearings, coolers, oil systems, controls, motors, and electricals.

03

Preventive Maintenance (AMC)

Scheduled maintenance plans designed to prevent failures and reduce energy costs. Benefits: longer equipment life, priority support, predictable costs, and detailed service records.

04

Compressor Overhauls

Complete restoration to near-new performance through precision inspection, machining, part replacement, and load testing.

05

Application-Specific Engineering

Custom compression solutions for low to high pressure applications (up to 100 bar), specialty gases, hazardous areas, and system integrations—engineered to your exact process needs.

06

Performance Optimization

Identify leaks, reduce excess pressure, optimize controls, and improve air quality. Typical energy savings: 10–30%

07

Retrofit & Upgrades

Modernize existing compressors with VSDs, advanced controllers, improved cooling, safety systems, and remote monitoring—without full replacement.

08

Air System Audits

Comprehensive audits to uncover energy losses and cost-saving opportunities, delivered with a clear improvement roadmap.

09

OEM-Equivalent Spare Parts

High-quality replacement spares for major brands including Ingersoll Rand, Atlas Copco, ELGI, Kirloskar, Chicago Pneumatic, KG Khosla, and Sullair—engineered for exact fit and reliable performance.
