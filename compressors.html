Compressor Types

Choose the Right Compression Technology

Different applications demand different compressor technologies. Explore our reciprocating and screw compressors to find the ideal solution for your operation.

Reciprocating Compressors

Ideal for high-pressure and intermittent duty applications, reciprocating compressors deliver durability and precise compressed air output using proven piston-cylinder technology.

Applications

  • High-pressure air systems (up to 40 bar)
  • PET bottle blowing
  • Instrument air systems
  • Pneumatic control & workshops

Key Features

  • Heavy-duty cast iron construction
  • Multi-stage configurations
  • Advanced intercooling
  • Low oil carryover
Capacity: 5–100 HP Pressure: 7–40 bar Cooling: Air / Water

Screw Compressors

Designed for 24/7 industrial operation, screw compressors provide stable, continuous airflow with minimal vibration, noise, and maintenance.

Applications

  • Continuous manufacturing operations
  • Pharmaceutical clean rooms
  • Food & beverage processing
  • Automotive production lines

Key Features

  • Oil-injected rotary screw technology
  • Integrated oil separation
  • Microprocessor controllers
  • Low noise & vibration
Capacity: 10–500 HP Pressure: 7–13 bar Drive: Direct / Belt
