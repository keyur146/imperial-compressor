About Us

Two decades of commitment to delivering dependable compression solutions.

Our Story

Imperial Compressors

Imperial Compressors Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2002 in Ahmedabad, India, with a vision to deliver dependable compression solutions built on engineering excellence. What began as a focused supplier of compressor spares has evolved into a comprehensive industrial solutions provider serving critical industries across the globe.

Since 2011, it is under a new management of young and visionary team. Imperial is engaged in the supply and export of industrial air and gas compressor parts based in the city of Ahmedabad.This transition marked a new chapter of growth, enabling us to invest in advanced manufacturing capabilities, expand our product portfolio, and strengthen our service infrastructure.

Imperial focuses on provide replacement parts for air compressors of reputed brands like Atlas Copco, Chicago Pneumatic, Kirloskar, KG Khosla, ELGI, Sullair and Ingersoll Rand.

Today, Imperial Compressors stands as a trusted partner for industries requiring uncompromising reliability in their compressed air and gas systems. Our commitment remains unchanged: deliver precision-engineered products backed by exceptional service.

Our Journey

From Vision to Industry Leadership

Key milestones that shaped our growth, capabilities, and commitment to reliable industrial solutions.

2002
Foundation

Company founded in Ahmedabad with focus on replacement spares for industrial compressors. Initial operations centered on customer pain points.

2007
Expansion

Expanded manufacturing capabilities and introduced complete compressor systems. Partnered with pharma & FMCG clients.

2011
Strategic Acquisition

Acquired by private equity investors enabling capital investment in advanced manufacturing and quality systems.

2015
ISO Certification

Achieved ISO certification, standardizing quality management systems across all operations.

2020
Global Footprint

Established operations in Lagos, Nigeria and expanded portfolio to premium compressor oils.

Today
Integrated Solutions Provider

Recognized as a comprehensive compression solutions partner offering products, services, and lifecycle support globally.
